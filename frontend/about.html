About SustainaBowl

Our Mission

Every meal has a story – not just about taste, but about impact. SustainaBowl helps you see the environmental cost of your food choices. The goal isn’t to make you quit your favorite dish, but to realize that even everyday habits play a part in shaping our planet’s future.

What We Do

Enter a dish, and we’ll show you its estimated carbon footprint. You’ll also get suggestions for eco-friendlier alternatives that keep nutritional values alive while lowering emissions.

Why It Matters

Food production contributes to climate change, deforestation, and water use. By making small, informed choices, we can collectively make a big difference.

Our Philosophy

We believe in awareness without guilt. No judgment, just knowledge – because the first step toward change is simply understanding the impact of our actions.
